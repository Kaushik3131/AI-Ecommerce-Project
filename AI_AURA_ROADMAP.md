## 🚀 Part 1: The Discovery Engine (Foundation)
The "Discovery Engine" allows users to find products based on meaning and visuals rather than just text.

### 1. Semantic Search
*   **Feature:** Natural language search (e.g., "warm lighting for a reading nook in a rainy city").
*   **Tech:** FastAPI + **Gemini 3 Flash** (for ultra-low latency embeddings).
*   **Benefit:** Zero "No results found." The AI understands the "vibe" and finds the closest matching intent.

### 2. Visual "Shop the Look"
*   **Feature:** User uploads a Pinterest post or a photo of a friend's house.
*   **Tech:** **Gemini 3 Flash** (Native Vision).
*   **Benefit:** Instant style detection (e.g., "Mid-century Modern," "Industrial Loft") and matching to our real-time Sanity inventory.
